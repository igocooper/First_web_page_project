Reasons to ride fixie

a fixie is..

Think: no coasting - that's often ridden without brakes.

A fixed-gear has at least as much stopping power as a beach cruiser
with a coaster brake. A fixed-gear has fewer parts to purchase and to
maintain. It's easy to balance in place without putting a foot
on the ground;

Then there's the security: A fixie has fewer parts to steal. And before
fixed-gears became ubiquitous, an unsuspecting thief probably
wouldn't make it to the end of the block without crashing.

Penny

Board

Penny boards are distinguished by a plastic deck, offered in an
array of colours.Due to their 'secret formula' the boards have very
interesting characteristics including its ability to survive the full
weight of a car and simply return to its original shape.Penny boards
are easier to carry around, compared to wooden skateboards.
Another unique characteristic of a Penny board is that they can be
ridden barefoot.

art of breaking

B-boying

or breaking, also called breakdancing, is a style of
street dance that originated primarily among African
American during the mid 1970s.The dance spread
worldwide due to popularity in the media. Although
the term "breakdance" is frequently used to refer to
the dance in popular culture and in the mainstream
entertainment industry, "b-boying" and "breaking"
are the original terms. These terms are preferred by
the majority of the pioneers and most
notable practitioners.
